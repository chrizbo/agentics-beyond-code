# Workflow Ideas — PM, Ops, Compliance, and GTM

Ideas for agentic workflows beyond code, sourced from product management and
product operations thought leaders. These are candidates for future
implementation as GitHub Agentic Workflows.

## Sources

- **Jenny Wanger** (jennywanger.com) — Product ops, AI workflow design, strategy systems
- **Chris Compston** (chriscompston.substack.com) — Product operating models, org transformation
- **Product Ops Confidential** (productopsconfidential.com) — Graham Reed & Antonia Landi
- **Medium /tag/product-management** — Trending PM articles (May 2026)
